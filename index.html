About the Manager

Ralph Trigg
Independent wealth manager

Ralph Trigg has spent more than three decades as an independent, fiduciary wealth manager serving families across the Coachella Valley and the broader American West. Based in La Quinta, California, his practice is built on a simple but increasingly rare principle: advice given in the client's best interest, without the conflicts of commission-based compensation or firm-mandated product recommendations.

His work focuses on the financial decisions that matter most — retirement income planning, Social Security timing, IRA and Roth strategy, estate coordination, Medicare planning, and the behavioral discipline that separates successful long-term investors from those who underperform their own portfolios. He has guided hundreds of families through the transition from accumulation to distribution.

As a fiduciary, Ralph operates under a legal standard that requires him to act in the client's interest — not his own. He charges no commissions, carries no affiliation with product companies, and has no incentive to recommend anything other than what is genuinely best for the person sitting across from him.
